GUANAJUATO

Got lost in irregular small allyways, climbed up steep staircase, and encountered amazing view from top of the hill.

2016.12 | MEXICO

Driving from Mexico City, Guanajuato was the second city we made a stop after Guadalajara. We parked our car in the lower level tunnel, walked our way up through narrow staircases, allowed ourselves to get lost in those irregularly allyways, until we accidently bumped into Plaza de la Paz, the center of the city.

We grabbed seats and drinks in a restaurant at Plaza de la Paz, made some sketches of the colonial architecture, enjoyed the sun and wind, and just looked at people walking by.
